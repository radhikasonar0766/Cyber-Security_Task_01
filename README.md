# Cyber-Security_Task_01_RadhikaSonar

### Personal Security Audit & Cyber Awareness Assessment

## About This Task
This task helps students understand the importance of cybersecurity in daily life. It focuses on personal security, password protection, online safety, and awareness of cyber threats.
Cybersecurity begins with protecting our own devices, accounts, and information before protecting networks and systems.

## Objectives
* Understand basic cybersecurity concepts.
* Analyze personal device security.
* Learn safe password practices.
* Check online account security.
* Study common cyber threats.
* Improve cyber awareness and safe internet habits.

## Task Contents
This project was completed to understand the importance of cybersecurity and safe digital practices in everyday life. The task involved examining personal device security settings, checking software updates, antivirus protection, and firewall status. It also focused on creating strong passwords, understanding Multi-Factor Authentication, and improving account security. Various online accounts were reviewed to identify security strengths and weaknesses. Important cyber threats such as phishing, malware, ransomware, social engineering, and data breaches were studied along with their prevention methods. A cyber awareness poster on "Safe Internet Usage" was designed to promote safe online behavior. Through this task, valuable knowledge about protecting personal information, maintaining digital privacy, and following secure internet practices was gained. The project highlights that cybersecurity is essential for everyone and that simple security measures can help protect devices, accounts, and data from cyber threats.

## Key Learnings
* Strong passwords protect online accounts.
* MFA provides additional security.
* Software updates fix security vulnerabilities.
* Cyber threats can affect anyone.
* Safe internet habits reduce online risks.

## Conclusion
Cybersecurity is an important skill for every computer user. This task helped improve awareness about digital safety and personal security practices. By following basic security measures, users can protect their devices, data, and online accounts from cyber threats.
